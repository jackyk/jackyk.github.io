My Story

I’m Jacky and I’m 22. I’m an autodidact web developer, a technology enthusiast and an aspiring hacker. I began my journey into tech a year ago and now teach at Tunapanda Institute in Kibera, Nairobi.

I have a knack for UI/UX development and testing. Interest and passion for software development and education. Impact oriented individual with the drive of problem solving and changing the little things I can in the world. Eager to be challenged in order to grow and improve my IT skills gained through previous experiences in the Technology and education sector
